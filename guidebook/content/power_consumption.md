# Power Usage

Electrical appliances can only be operated if they are in the power grid and the power generated by the grid is not less than the power consumed.

The *Heater*'s power consumption is 8KW. It heats the block above and allows the anvil to perform smelting operations.

<Recipe id="anvilcraft:heater" />

The *Auto Crafter*'s power consumption is 1KW. It is an enhanced version of the vanilla crafter, with filtering options. It works 64 times every 1 second when receiving a continuous redstone signal.

<Recipe id="anvilcraft:batch_crafter" />

The *Power Converter*'s power consumption is 1KW, 3KW or 9KW respectively accroding to the size. It can provide FE/RF/AE energy to the block it is attached to. Each KW of power produces 80FE/t, but there is a 10% loss (The power conversion efficiency and the loss can be adjusted in the configuration file).
