<a name="ember_metal"></a>
<ItemImage id="anvilcraft:ember_metal_ingot" />

*Ember Metal* is a very hot metal. 
Use time warp with any *heated Netherite Blocks* to craft this. You can add 1-3 *Earth Core Shard* to increase result.

*Multiphase Matter* is an artificial matter.

(This will be Anvil Collision recipe, until it has support)Put a [Frost Metal Block](#frost_metal_block_and_nugget) on the tail of Collision facility and do it once to craft the Multiphase Matter.

<ItemImage id="tag:anvilcraft:multiple_to_one_smithing_templates" />
