# Special Tool Properties

This page shows all available *Special Tool Properties* and their affections.

<a name="merciless"></a>
## Merciless

Only included in [Frost Metal tools](../smithing_system/smithing_tier_2_materials.md#frost_metal_tools) by default. 
The item included this property will loss all enchantments' affection except *Durability*、*Mending*、*Riptide*、*Royalty*. But every level of these enchantments will provide 1 attack damage and 1 mining efficiency.

<a name="reforging"></a>
## Fire Reforging

Only included in [Ember Metal tools](../smithing_system/smithing_tier_2_materials.md#ember_metal_tools) and [Multiphase Matter tools](../smithing_system/smithing_tier_2_materials.md#multiphase_matter_tools) by default. 
The item included this property will mend 2 damages every tick in the fire. Soul fire will be 5, and lava (cauldron) will be 10.

<a name="multiphase"></a>
## Multiphase

Only included in [Multiphase Matter tools](../smithing_system/smithing_tier_2_materials.md#multiphase_matter_tools) by default. 
The item included this property will have two "phases". Every phase will store their own name and enchantments, no affection with each other, and can switch by pressing [<KeyBind id="key.anvilcraft.switch_phase" />] (your "Switch Phase" keybinding.)
