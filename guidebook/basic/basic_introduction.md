# Basic Introduction

AnvilCraft is a mod that triggers effects by falling an anvil on a block, item or mob.
Placing different blocks underneath the anvil will trigger different effects. You can automate the production or recycle some items in this way. 
In order to facilitate the lifting of the anvil, the mod adds the *Magnet Block*. You can use vanilla redstone machinery or manual operation instead, but it is more complicated.


The drop of the anvil can also be used to generate electricity and even power the electrical appliances from other mods. Based on this, this mod adds a power system and machines. $(be)Anvils have a lot of room for improvement in enchantment and forging, and this mod adds forging gameplay to increase the enchantment cap.
These two parts are covered in dedicated sections.

$(/be)
