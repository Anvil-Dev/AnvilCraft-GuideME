# Power Introduction

The energy of this mod is power-based. If the power generated in the power grid is greater than or equal to the power consumed, all electrical appliances will work normally. Otherwise, it is overloaded and cannot work. The power grid is wireless in this mod, and the power supply range is provided by transmission poles, which connect generators and electrical appliances to the grid. The power unit is KW, which can be analogous to the actual kilowatt, but there is no necessary connection.

The *Magnetoelectric Core* is a key component of synthetic power-related machines.

The *Anvil Hammer* is the wrench in this mod. You can debug the direction of some blocks with it, and dismantle some blocks with it when holding shift. At the same time, it is an anvil. Left-clicking on a block with it is same as the block being hit by a anvil.

<Recipe id="anvilcraft:anvil_hammer" />

It is also a hammer, which drops attacking mobs from a height and adds damage to mobs as from an anvil falling. Also a goggles, which can be worn on the head to view the grid range and grid load info. It is also a head mallet, and when worn on the head, the elytra fly and hit mobs to deal damage. Note that left-clicking on a block, left-clicking and head-mallet hitting a mob will consume the anvil hammer's durability, but other operations will not.
